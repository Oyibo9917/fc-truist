Banking for the education sector

You help students meet their full potential.

Well help your school do it, too.

Partner with us to manage your future growth.

Academic and technological advances provide constantly changing opportunities for K-12 independent schools and higher education. Weve supported schools for more than 100 yearsand have the industry knowledge and financial expertise to support your school in debt capital, treasury, risk management, and investments.

Our local bankers, partnered with our industry consultants, will help you evaluate strategies so your school meets its long-term growth plans and aspirations.

Areas of focus

  • Public and private higher education, including direct support organizations
  • Independent K-12 schools
  • Charter schools
