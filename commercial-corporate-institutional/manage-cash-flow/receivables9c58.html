Lockbox

Accurate. Speedy. Secure. Simplify your data collection process with innovative solutions and better technology. Reduce your company’s dependency on paper. Consolidate your processes. And improve your bottom line. All with four complete Lockbox solutions—Medical Lockbox, Wholesale Lockbox, Retail Lockbox, and Electronic Lockbox. 

Component ID : "accordionGridLayout-1586858373"
Model : "stack"
Position : "left"

Benefits

  • Access multiple pickup schedules, quick delivery of incoming payments, and unique postal codes speed up account posting and maximize your cash flow.
  • Automate processes to increase your working capital.
  • Receive consumer payments faster and decrease days outstanding for payment reconcilement.
  • Enhance secure information management with multiple levels of access and security.
  • Reallocate employee resources to revenue-generating tasks.

Best fit if you

  • Medical Lockbox helps manage your medical information, simplifying your medical filing process.
  • Wholesale Lockbox processes high-dollar, corporate-to-corporate payments.
  • Retail Lockbox is best for companies receiving a large volume of low-dollar consumer-to-corporate payments.
  • Electronic Lockbox is best for companies that receive a high volume of consumer payments through online channels.

Questions about Lockbox? Contact your treasury consultant or relationship manager.

Integrated Receivables

Faster, more convenient, and less expensive than manually processing checks or wires. Streamline your accounts receivable processes, reduce the risk of fraud, and increase payment accuracy by delivering detailed and consolidated transaction and remittance data.

Component ID : "accordionGridLayout-1938807323"
Model : "stack"
Position : "left"

Benefits

  • Boost efficiency by processing results from various receivables systems.
  • Lower administrative, audit, and collection costs.
  • Increase security and customize capabilities by managing user entitlements and control access.
  • Get fast alerts via email when important actions or situations occur.
  • Save time by eliminating delays caused by customer check production and mail delivery, shortening receivables posting time, and reducing the number of days sales are outstanding. 

Best fit if you

  • Manage multiple types of receivables, including checks, ACH, wire transfers, and lockbox data.

Questions about Integrated Receivables? Contact your treasury consultant or relationship manager.
