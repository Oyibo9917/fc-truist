Thereafter, we will use the highest average monthly balance of the 3 most previous months to determine your annual fee. Annual fee tiers are defined as follows: a) highest 3 month average balance of less than $25,000 is charged an annual fee of $120 b) highest 3 month average balance of $25,000 and less than $50,000 is charged an annual fee of $90 and c) highest 3 month average balance of $50,000 or more is charged an annual fee of $60. Annual fee will be calculated at the end of the month prior to the annual fee being charged. For all other Business Checking accounts, the annual fee is $120.
